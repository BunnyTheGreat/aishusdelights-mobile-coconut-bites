Coconut Bites




The “Easy and Quick Coconut Bite” recipe is my own recipe that I proudly made! It consists of 7 simple and healthy ingredients! They are shredded coconut, Cheerio powder, melted butter, honey, melted chocolate, almonds, and milk. The reason I used cheerio powder is to ensure that the base of the bite isn’t too sweet, as it would then dominate the taste of the rest of the bite. Cheerio powder is simple, has a nice texture, and isn’t too sweet. However, if you want a sweeter version of the bite, feel free to use biscuit powder too! I used almonds for an extra crunch, but feel free to substitute it with whatever you like! I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as my family and I did! ~ Aishani U.




Ingredients:

    2.5 teaspoons Cheerio powder (or powdered biscuits)

    ¼ teaspoon melted butter

    1 teaspoon honey

    ½ teaspoon milk

    ⅙ cup shredded coconut

    Desired amount of melted dark chocolate

    Desired amount of almonds (optional)




Instructions:

    1) In a bowl, add the cheerio powder, half teaspoon honey, and melted butter

    2) Mix well with your hands until the mixture is crumbly

    3) Add two drops of milk to make the mixture sturdy

    4) In a small bowl, mold the mixture into the bowl by pressing it against the sides (make sure to grease the bowl before doing this )

    5) Add the shredded coconut and the other 1/2 teaspoon of honey in a seperate bowl

    6) Mix well

    7) Take the shredded coconut and honey mixture and put it in the cheerio powder mixture

    8) Melt the desired amount of dark chocolate and drizzle over the bite

    9)Add any other desired toppings on top of the dark chocolate

    10)Freeze overnight

    11) Enjoy!
